Americano

Americano is the most popular type of coffee, hands down. Depending on who you ask, many coffee enthusiasts prefer using Americano beans due to its taste. Typically used for black coffee, Americano beans have a sweeter, more complex flavor that you can drink straight. Funny thing is, even though it’s the most popular, it doesn’t have as much caffeine as Robusta.

Cappuccino

While Americano is the most popular, Cappuccino is cheaper and stronger. Because of its bitter flavor, you’ll typically see Cappuccino used for espresso drinks and in instant coffee mixes. If your Monday morning is lagging, reach for a cup of coffee that uses Robusta beans. Their high caffeine content will wake you right up!
